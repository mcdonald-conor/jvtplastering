My Services

First Fix Plastering

I start with the essential groundwork that you might not see but forms the foundation of a quality finish. I'll handle everything from plasterboard installation to backing coats, ensuring your surfaces are perfectly prepared for that final smooth finish.

Second Fix Plastering

This is where I bring your walls to life - creating that perfect visible finish that transforms your space. From smooth skimming over existing walls to achieving that flawless finish you'll see every day.

External Rendering

I'll protect and enhance your property's exterior with my rendering expertise. Whether you need traditional sand and cement or modern render systems, I'll help weatherproof your home while improving its appearance.

Repairs & Renovations

From fixing cracks and damage to complete room renovations - no job is too small. I'll help restore your walls to their former glory or give them a complete makeover.
